Recommendation

  • Default: V3 (block above options). Review summary is the clearest differentiator SA can offer over a Shopify-native PDP — putting it above the fold honors that. The dedicated block gives the text summary visual authority and doesn't blend into the product description.
  • V5 (quote-style) is the editorial alternative if merchants prefer softer visuals. Functionally equivalent to V3 but lighter.
  • Avoid V1 (plain paragraph). Summary visually merges with the description. Shoppers won't realize it's aggregated review content.
  • Avoid V4 (below description) unless we pair with a V2-style truncation elsewhere. Too easy to miss.
  • V6 (bottom band) is worth A/B testing — reassurance-at-decision-point is a known e-commerce pattern, but it's unconventional here.
  • Open: how long should the summary be? Current draft is 2–3 sentences (~30 words). Anything longer hurts PDP density. Anything shorter loses the "texture" that makes it useful.
  • Open: how is the summary generated and refreshed? Live at PDP open, cached per product, nightly batch? Latency target <500ms for PDP open.
