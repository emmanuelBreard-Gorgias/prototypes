Recommendation

  • Default: V2 (inline with link). Lowest footprint, rating is always visible above-fold, and the count is tappable for shoppers who want to read reviews on the storefront. Matches how shoppers already read a Shopify PDP.
  • If we want a stronger signal: V4 (block above options). Worth testing against V2 for high-consideration categories (beauty, supplements, skincare) where reviews drive conversion. Expect higher tap-through to storefront reviews, minor tradeoff on PDP scan speed.
  • Avoid V5 (block below description). Adds weight without the above-fold benefit. Only viable with a V3-style title badge.
  • Consider V6 (bottom bar) if we want the trust signal near the "Add to cart" moment. A/B candidate against V4.
  • Open: where does "See all" go? Recommend opening the storefront product page anchored to its reviews section in a new tab. Keeps SA in the chat, lets the shopper dive deep if they want.
  • Open: quick replies appear regardless of rating placement. Confirm that rating + suggestions don't compete for attention — if they do, V2 is the safest since rating is a single line.
