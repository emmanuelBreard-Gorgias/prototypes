In-chat PDP — Review Summary Placements

Three ways to surface an AI-generated review summary on the in-chat PDP, ordered by how much they assert themselves: V3 (dedicated summary block above the subscribe options), V10 (headline always visible, per-topic detail one click away), and V12 (most subtle: a small rating line with the entire summary, headline and topics, folded behind one toggle). All show the rating signal and a short text summary of what reviewers are saying. No "See all" CTA. Each renders the full PDP including left-aligned quick-reply pills to show how components compete for attention.

Recommendation

  • Placement: V3 (block above options). Review summary is the clearest differentiator SA can offer over a Shopify-native PDP — putting it above the fold honors that. The dedicated block gives the text summary visual authority and doesn't blend into the product description.
  • Content depth: V10 (headline + click to expand aspects, no color). The headline keeps momentum for scanners, and neutral per-topic detail stays one tap away, off the card face, with no colored dots that could read as a caution flag on the merchant's product. Always pass the full aspect structure to SA so it answers per-aspect questions in conversation.
  • Most subtle: V12 (tiny rating, full summary folded). For merchants who want the lightest footprint, or as the low-disclosure arm of an A/B test. Trade-off: the summary is invisible until a shopper expands it, so we lose the at-a-glance differentiator. Worth testing whether the smaller signal still lifts conversion or just disappears.
  • Open: how long should the summary be? Current draft is 2–3 sentences (~30 words). Anything longer hurts PDP density. Anything shorter loses the "texture" that makes it useful.
  • Open: how is the summary generated and refreshed? Live at PDP open, cached per product, nightly batch? Latency target <500ms for PDP open.
