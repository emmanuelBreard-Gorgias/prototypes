Recommendation

  • Default: V2 (inline + link). Lowest cost, rating always visible, full reviews are one tap away. Matches how Shopify-native PDPs feel.
  • Investment case: V6 (accordion). If we want shoppers to engage with review content without leaving the PDP, the accordion is the cleanest trade-off. Worth testing against V2.
  • Avoid V5 (tabs) unless we also show a rating badge in the title area. Hiding the rating behind a tab defeats the purpose of showing social proof on the PDP.
  • V3 (block above options) only for merchants where reviews are the primary conversion lever (niche DTC, high-consideration products). Test per-merchant, don't default.
  • Open: where does "See all" navigate to? Dedicated reviews sub-screen inside the drawer (back arrow to PDP) vs. external link to the storefront product page. Sub-screen keeps shoppers in flow.
