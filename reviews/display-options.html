Open questions to resolve before shipping

  • Data source. Confirm metafield coverage across top review apps (Judge.me, Yotpo, Okendo, Stamped). If coverage <80%, plan per-app integrations.
  • Threshold. Hide rating below N reviews? Recommend N=5; a 5.0 (1) hurts trust more than helps.
  • Empty state. Confirm we hide entirely (no "No reviews yet" text). Avoid negative signal on new SKUs.
  • Card pick. Recommend V1 (classic stars + number + count) for the carousel — most readable at 160px, lowest ambiguity. Fallback to V2 if vertical space becomes a problem.
  • Drawer pick. Recommend D2 (summary block) — best ROI on the extra space without D3's weight. D3 only if we can link to a full review list below.
